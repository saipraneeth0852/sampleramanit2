About Raman iT

Beyond boxes.
Beyond people.

“ICT products and services should be liberated — and looked at beyond mere boxes and people.”

Raman iT brings nearly three decades of IT consultation, system integration and managed services to enterprises across India. Headquartered in Mysuru with a corporate office in Bengaluru, we are a single-stop destination for every layer of IT infrastructure — in the data center and in the cloud.

With a strength of 100+ employees, our trust is earned through stringent quality processes, compliance policies and on-time delivery across every project — recognized by industry awards along the way. We serve customers across IT & ITES, education, healthcare, research, manufacturing, government, banking, airports, temples, railways, nuclear establishments and defense.
